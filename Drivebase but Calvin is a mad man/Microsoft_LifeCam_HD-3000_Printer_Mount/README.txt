# Summary

Simple bracket to attach Hd-3000 webcam to 6mm framed 3d printer (or, well, whatever you like that is 6mm thick really). 

I used the instructions here to open camera and replace stock bracket: https://astrophotovideo.wordpress.com/adapting-a-webcam-to-a-telescope/
